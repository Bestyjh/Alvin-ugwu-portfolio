Alvin is a dedicated educator and researcher passionate about advancing Education for Sustainability through innovative curriculum design and experiential learning. With a strong academic foundation that includes a Master of Education (Summa Cum Laude) and a Bachelor of Education (Honours) from the University of KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa, Alvin's work bridges theory and practice to promote transformative learning experiences.

His professional experience as a graduate research and teaching assistant reflects a strong commitment to inclusive and sustainable educational practices.
